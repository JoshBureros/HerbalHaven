Yerba Buena

Yerba Buena (Clinopodium douglasii)

Yerba Buena is a perennial herb belonging to the mint family, widely known in the Philippines for its medicinal properties. It is commonly used in traditional medicine for its soothing, analgesic, and aromatic qualities.

Medicinal Uses:

  • Pain Relief: Leaves can be pounded and used as a poultice for headaches, toothaches, and body aches.
  • Stomach Ache: Tea made from the leaves can help soothe abdominal pain, indigestion, and gas.
  • Cold and Cough: The minty aroma helps clear nasal passages and ease sore throats when used as tea or steam inhalation.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces swelling and muscle pain.
  • Antibacterial: Useful for treating minor wounds due to its natural antibacterial effect.
  • Relaxant: Yerba Buena tea may reduce stress and promote relaxation.

This plant is recognized by the Philippine Department of Health as an approved medicinal herb. For serious conditions, it's still best to consult a medical professional.
