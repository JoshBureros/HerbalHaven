Medicinal plants

Garlic

Scientific Name: Allium sativum

Garlic is one of the most widely used herbal remedies and cooking ingredients around the world. It is known for its distinctive taste and numerous health benefits.

Medicinal Benefits:

  • Boosts the immune system
  • Reduces blood pressure
  • Lowers cholesterol levels
  • Fights infections and colds
  • Has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties

Garlic can be consumed raw, cooked, or as a supplement. It has been used in traditional medicine for centuries and is especially popular in heart and immune system care.
