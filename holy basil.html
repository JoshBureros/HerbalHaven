Holy Basil

Holy Basil (Ocimum sanctum / Tulsi)

Holy Basil, also known as Tulsi, is a sacred herb in India and widely used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine. It is revered for its healing properties and spiritual significance.

Medicinal Uses:

  • Respiratory Health: Relieves symptoms of asthma, bronchitis, and colds through its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties.
  • Stress Relief: Acts as an adaptogen that helps the body cope with stress and anxiety.
  • Immunity Booster: Enhances the immune response and helps protect against infections.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces inflammation and pain in the body.
  • Blood Sugar Regulation: Supports healthy blood sugar levels and is helpful for managing diabetes.
  • Antibacterial and Antiviral: Fights a variety of infections naturally.
  • Digestive Aid: Improves digestion and alleviates bloating or indigestion.
  • Skin Health: Used in skincare for acne and wound healing due to its purifying properties.

Holy Basil is commonly consumed as herbal tea, supplements, or used fresh in remedies. While generally safe, it should be used under proper guidance if you have health conditions or are pregnant.
